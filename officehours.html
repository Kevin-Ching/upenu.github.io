{% include section-header.html title="Mock Interviews" %}

During our professional hours, UPE offers free mock technical interviews to the general public. Each interview lasts 30 minutes and is hosted by one of our Professional Development committee members. Interiews are primarily whiteboard coding interviews, but we can accomodate others by request. We also provide post-interview critique on how to do better the next time around.

Walk-in hours are every day from 1-2pm. Otherwise, mock interviews are available by appointment only! To sign up, please visit us here

{% include section-header.html title="Resume Critique" %}

In addition to mock interviews, during our professional hours, we also provide free resume critique to the general public. We request a paper copy of your resume to make critiquing easier. One of our Professional Development committee members will provide personalized advice and critique based on their experiences. Each session can last between 15-30 minutes depending on current availability.

These are available via walk in. Walk-in hours are every day from 1-2pm. . Please check the schedule here for professional hours.
