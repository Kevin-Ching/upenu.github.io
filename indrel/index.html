{% include section-header.html title="Resume Book" %}

The Berkeley UPE Resume Book contains resumes of our members, who are in the top third of the declared computer science student community at UC Berkeley. Our resume book is updated every semester and includes resumes from our alumni, graduate students, and undergraduate students (mostly seniors and juniors). Currently, we offer it in the form of a downloadable PDF at the cost of $200.

If you have any questions or would like to purchase the resume book, please email ir (at) upe.berkeley.edu with "UPE Resume Book Purchase" as the subject line.
