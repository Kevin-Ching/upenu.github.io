Upsilon Pi Epsilon is the international honor society for the Computing Sciences with the goal of recognizing academic excellence in the Computing and Information Disciplines. UPE provides academic and professional development support to the UC Berkeley community as well as providing its members with a community to help them achieve their true potential.
